# Endfed Antennas, QRM, and How to Improve Your Reception

> 来源: PA9X (Jean-Paul), pa9x.com
> 日期: 2025-08-27
> 摘要: TX/RX 分离架构 —— EFHW 发射 + 有源磁环接收

## 核心观点

1. EFHW 天线对人为噪声 (QRM) 高度敏感——这是其最大弱点
2. 「被听到但听不到回复」是 EFHW 用户的典型挫折
3. EFHW 不是坏天线——它在发射端表现优秀，但接收需要辅助
4. 解决方案：TX用EFHW，RX用有源磁环天线

## 推荐方案

- **发射**: 保持现有 EFHW + 49:1 变压器
- **接收**: 添加有源磁环天线（Active Magnetic Loop）
  - MLA-30+ (中国产，预算之选)
  - Wellgood Loop v4 (M1GEO, DIY方案)
  - W6LVP 完整方案 (专业级)
- **切换**: T/R Switch 自动切换 (QRO.cz / 电台内置 RX ANT 接口)

## 磁环核心优势

- 尖锐的方向性 null —— 对准噪声源即可消除
- 噪声基底显著降低
- 体积小(< 1m)，适合城市/郊区

## 原始文章摘要

The endfed antenna has become one of the most popular choices among amateur radio operators in recent years. Its ability to cover multiple bands and its easy installation often make it the first antenna many radio amateurs put up. But there is one major drawback: the endfed antenna is highly sensitive to man-made noise (QRM).

The endfed is especially attractive in places where space is limited. Unfortunately, that often means installing it in urban or suburban environments, where electrical devices generate plenty of interference. Because of its electrical characteristics, the endfed antenna readily picks up this noise.

Solution: transmit with the endfed, receive with the magnetic loop. The magnetic loop's sharp null allows you to rotate the null toward a noise source, significantly reducing interference.

Most radio amateurs agree that the magnetic loop antenna is one of the best solutions for receiving in high-QRM areas. With diameters up to about 1 meter (3 ft), these active receiving loops are small, effective, and easy to add alongside your transmitting antenna.
