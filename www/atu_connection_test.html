Troubleshooting Guide

  • Make sure your ATR-1000 is powered on
  • Verify the IP address (192.168.1.12) is correct
  • Check that port 81 is not blocked by firewall
  • Ensure your device is on the same network
  • Try accessing http://192.168.1.12 in your browser
